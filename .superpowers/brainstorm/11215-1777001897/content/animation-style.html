What animation style fits the site?

Choose the vibe — this sets the tone for all motion on the site

A

Subtle & Professional

Gentle fade-ins on scroll, smooth hover lifts on cards, soft glow on focus. Nothing showy — every animation serves clarity. Think Vercel, Linear, Stripe. Employers won't be distracted; the work speaks.

B

Atmospheric & Immersive

Ambient gradient blobs drifting behind cards, particle field in the hero, glowing card borders on hover. Moody and cinematic — the kind of site that gets bookmarked. More impressive, higher complexity.

C

Kinetic & Bold

Staggered entrance animations, hero text sliding in letter-by-letter, bento cards cascading into view on load, parallax scroll effects. High-impact — awwwards-tier presentation. Highest effort and risk of feeling heavy.
